Introduction

Lux Umbra is a 3D puzzle game. The goal is to finish the level by reaching the door. For that you have to deal with obstacles on your way.

Story

The small player is living in a small village near the Sun-valley. One Day through an accident the darkness contained by the original maya spreads in his village more and more people develope negative character traits and soon the first village people fully give in to the darkness. The Player decides climb the Sun-and Fun-mountain hoping the rumors about its magic are true and the illuminating capabilities the climbers can gain.

Accompany small player on his magical journey through sunvalley and solve the puzzles to climb the mountain.

Gameplay

Player will follow a unique way predetermined by his ancestors to determine if he is worthy. The magic inherited by the sacrifices of the maya and their unique connection to the light and shadows is conserved in a few obstacle and awakens its full power when coming in touch with a descendant of the mayas. The mechanism to get on top of the mountain range from basic pressure plates to portals and lightboxes. The LightBox is the center piece of maya magic, even in the darkest hours it will emit a unique beam of light. On interaction with small player throws a shadow with whom the player can change places. The range of the lightbeam therefore gives the distance, in which the shadow will appear.
