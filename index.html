Story

The player is living in a small village near the Sun Valley. One day all of the sudden the darkness contained by the original maya spreads in his village. More and more people develope negative character traits and soon the first village people give in to the darkness. The player decides to climb the 'Sun and Fun Mountain' hoping the rumors about its magic are true and he can bring the light back to his small village.

Accompany the player on his magical journey through the Sun Valley and solve the puzzles to climb the mountain.

Gameplay

The player will follow in the footsteps of his ancestors to determine if he is worthy. The magic inherited by the sacrifices of the Maya and their unique connection to the light and shadows is preserved in Mayan relics. They awaken their full power when coming in touch with a descendant of the mayas. The tools the player will use to climb to the peak range from basic pressure plates all the way to magic portals. The lightsource is the core pillar of Mayan magic. Even in the darkest hours it will emit a fantastical beam of light. When the light falls upon the player, a shadow is casted, to which the player can teleport.

Items

A player and other items with different features can be found in the game. In the following section, the most important ones are shown and explained.

Gamefigure

Lucia is the hero of the story for Lux Et Umbra. She wants to save her village and people from eternal darkness and will do anything for them! During the game, Lucia will learn about her mystical shadow power that helps her overcome even the most difficult obstacles. Together with the ancient Mayan light sources, she can use her shadow to teleport over large gaps and distances. With all of these powers, nothing can stand between Lucia and the top of the 'Sun and Fun' mountain!

Portal

The portal is an item that teleports the player and other movable objects. If something moves or is pushed through the portal, it gets teleported to its connected portal. It cannot be moved, but things can pass through the portal from both sides. The shadow can also be teleported.

Mirror

The Mirror can reflect the laser and subsequently change its direction. Depending on how the mirror is rotated it can bend the laser in a 90Â° angle to the right or left. If light falls upon the player that has been reflected by a mirror, the distance to the players shadow will depend on the distance to the mirror instead of the light source.

Pressure Plate

The pressure plate is essential to finishing all levels, as it opens the door and allows the player to progress. It can be activated by the player or any other object, however in order to keep the door open, it must remain activated.

Light Source

The light source is one of the most important items in this game. It allows the player to teleport across gaps with the help of the mystical shadow power.
